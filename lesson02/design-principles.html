Design Principles Document

Delilia Werner

Rule of Thirds

Family Locket

The website Family Locket uses the Rule of Thirds by positioning their podcast image and the title of the podcast in the intersections of the grid so it will be more visually appealing.

Visual Heirarchy

Herbal Remedies Advice

The website Herbal Remedies Advice uses visual heirarchy to emphasize the message that the herbalist wishes her audience to understand.

White Space and Clean Design

Your DNA Guide

The website Your DNA Guide uses white space and clean design to separate the items on its site to help the eyes focus and the brain comprehend what is being displayed.
